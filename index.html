HOW TO PLAY

01
BUILD YOUR COMBO
Kill enemies quickly to build your COMBO multiplier — each kill earns more points!
02
BOSS WAVES
Bosses appear every 3rd wave. Use the Sniper for headshots — they deal 1.5× damage.
03
COLLECT POWERUPS
Enemies drop health crates and ammo boxes. Walk over them to collect.
04
SWITCH WEAPONS
Use Q or Scroll Wheel to cycle between Rifle, Shotgun, and Sniper.
05
SPRINT TO DODGE
Hold SHIFT to sprint — use it to dodge enemy fire and reposition fast.
06
USE GRENADES
Press G to throw grenades for massive area damage. They recharge over time.
07
PERFORMANCE TIP
Experiencing lag? Disable "High Quality Mode" in the Settings menu for a smoother experience.
